How It Works

Order from any of the food trucks in Red Square in four easy steps:

  • 1) Click on a food truck to view the available menus.
  • 2) Make your selections.
  • 3) Submit and confirm your order. Your order will be put into the queue.
  • 4) Go and claim your food when it is ready.

About Us

We are four juniors at the University of Washington who have soaked in the rain to get our lunches for two years too many. We created this website to expedite the process of ordering and receiving meals, while keeping the chances of slipping across Red Square to a minimum.
